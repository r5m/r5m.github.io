    Welcome to R5M Finance project

    Full featured HTML5 cross platform finance manager:

    • Multiple accounts
    • Multiple currencies
    • Backups and synchronization with your Dropbox account
    • Works offline
    • Cross-platform: Apk version for Android and full featured "online" version (doesn't require internet connection) for other devices
    • Russian & English locales

    System requirements

    Apk version requires Android 4.0.3+.
    Online version has all the features of Android one.
    The only requirement is HTML5 web-browser with touch gestures, local storage, indexedDB/WebSQL, font-family support (almost every modern smartphone).
